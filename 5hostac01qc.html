Results from the Hot Spot Analysis, see Fig shows that 541 out of 2844 (19%) of all East Harlem properties are within the 90-99% confidence level. More specifically 176 out of 2844 (6%) are at 95% and 185 out of 2844 (7%) are at 99%. This means that 19% of all residential units in East Harlem have a high degree of vulnerability to coastal flooding and the three socio-economic indicators included in this study.
